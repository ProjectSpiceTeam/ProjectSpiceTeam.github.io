About ProjectSpice

ProjectSpice is a versatile Discord bot designed to enhance your cooking experience! manage ingredients, cook recipes, and interact with fellow cooking enthusiasts all in one place.

Features

  • Order some delicious food
  • Apply for chef and cook food for the customers
  • Fun rewards and achievements

Commands

Here are just a few of the commands you can use with ProjectSpice:

  • /daily special - view the daily special
  • /help - get a list of all commands
  • /discord - get a link to the Discord
  • /menu - get a link to the menu
